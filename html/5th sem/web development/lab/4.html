SEPM- Software Engineering And Project Management

Software Engineering and Project Management is taken by Prof. Maria
Software Engineering involves designing, developing, and maintaining software systems to meet user needs and technical specifications.
Project Management in this context focuses on planning, executing, and closing software projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to stakeholders' satisfaction.
Together, they ensure the successful delivery of high-quality software products.

CN- Computer Networks

CN- Computer Networks is taken by Prof. Swathy
A computer network is a system that connects multiple computing devices to share resources and information.
These devices can include computers, servers, printers, and more, and they communicate through wired or wireless connections.
Networks enable efficient data exchange, supporting applications like email, file sharing, and internet browsing.

TOC- Theory Of Computation

TOC- Theory Of Computation is taken by Prof. Rakesh
The Theory of Computation is a branch of computer science that explores how efficiently problems can be solved using algorithms on computational models.
It encompasses three main areas:
automata theory and languages,computability theory, and computational complexity theory.
This field helps us understand the fundamental limits of what can be computed and how efficiently it can be done.

CG- Computer Graphics

CG- Computer Graphics is taken by Prof. PriyaDarshini
Computer graphics is a technology that generates images on computer screens, used in digital photography, film, video games, and electronic devices.
It involves creating, manipulating, and displaying visual content using computers. This field includes both 2D and 3D graphics, enhancing user experiences across various media.
